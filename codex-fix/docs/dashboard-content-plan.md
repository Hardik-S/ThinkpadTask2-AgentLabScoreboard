# Local Agent Lab Scoreboard Content Plan

## Dashboard Sections

1. **Overview**
   - Introduction to the project, including its goals and objectives.
   - Overview of the local Qwen setup and its role in the experiment.

2. **Experiment Details**
   - Description of the Codex-supervised experiment.
   - Explanation of the worker/model (ThinkPad e530 running Qwen 2.5-coder:3b).
   - Turn budget details, including total turns and maximum time per turn.

3. **Performance Metrics**
   - Summary of the performance metrics for the experiment.
   - Breakdown of attempted, completed, failed, and timed-out attempts.

4. **Prompt Shape Analysis**
   - Detailed analysis of the prompt shapes used in the experiment.
   - Explanation of the strong and weak prompt shapes, including their impact on performance.

5. **Artifact Quality**
   - Discussion of the quality of the raw artifacts generated during the experiment.
   - Key areas for improvement in artifact quality.

6. **Codex Repair Burden**
   - Overview of the repair burden associated with the Codex model.
   - Explanation of specific issues and their resolution.

7. **Validation Issues**
   - Description of validation issues encountered during the experiment.
   - Steps taken to address these issues, including the use of a raw validator script.

8. **Recommendations**
   - Final recommendations for improving the local Qwen setup and workflow.
   - Suggestions for future experiments and improvements.

## Reviewer Story

- **Introduction**
  - Brief overview of the reviewer's role in the experiment.
  - Explanation of their responsibilities and expectations.

- **Experiment Review Process**
  - Detailed explanation of the review process, including how performance metrics were evaluated.
  - Discussion of any discrepancies or areas for improvement identified during the review.

- **Key Findings**
  - Summary of key findings from the experiment.
  - Highlighted strengths and weaknesses in the local Qwen setup and workflow.

- **Recommendations for Improvement**
  - Final recommendations based on the reviewer's observations.
  - Suggestions for future experiments and improvements.

---

This content plan provides a structured framework for the static dashboard sections and reviewer story, ensuring clarity and completeness.
