### Dashboard Copy Polish Recommendations

1. **Title**: Update the title of the dashboard to reflect its purpose more clearly and concisely. For example, "Agent Lab Scoreboard" could be changed to "Performance Metrics".

2. **Introduction**: Add a brief introduction that explains what the dashboard is intended for and how it can help stakeholders understand the performance of the agent lab.

3. **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**:
   - **Turn Completion Rate**: Highlight the percentage of turns completed successfully.
   - **Failed or Timed Out Turns**: Mention the number of turns that failed or timed out, along with a brief explanation if necessary.
   - **Average Turn Time**: Provide an average turn time to give context on how quickly tasks are being processed.

4. **Charts and Graphs**:
   - **Turn Completion Over Time**: Use a line chart to show the trend of turn completion over time, highlighting any significant changes or periods of high activity.
   - **Failed vs. Completed Turns**: A bar chart could be used to visually compare the number of failed turns versus completed ones.

5. **Performance Metrics**:
   - **Average Response Time**: Include a metric for average response time to indicate how quickly tasks are being processed on average.
   - **Error Rate**: Show the error rate to help identify any recurring issues or areas for improvement.

6. **Actionable Insights**:
   - Provide actionable insights based on the data, such as suggesting improvements to turn completion rates or identifying potential bottlenecks.
   - Include a call-to-action (CTA) if there are specific actions that can be taken based on the dashboard's findings.

7. **Footer**: Add a footer with contact information and links to relevant resources or documentation for further assistance.
